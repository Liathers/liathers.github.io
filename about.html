About

I'm a Minecaft and Programming enthusiast, and spend a lot of my time researching and messing around with legacy versions of the game, as well as making the odd programming project.

I host a Survival Multiplayer Server for a small group of people to play with few restrictions. There are no mods installed directly onto the server, things such as geyser are all handled through a Velocity proxy, meaning the server can be updated as soon as there is an update to the game. The map has been reset twice, and now is on a Long-Term world.
